Corporate -Storytelling . One of the other intriguing things we hope to perfect at UPTOPIA is the art of telling beautiful stories -words that commutate ideas or philosophies that make brands and people be and look better . When businesses churn out products and or services,they instinctively seek out ways to better represent themselves in the eyes of stakeholders, exactly how this pans out is the stuff of corporate storytelling. A renowned sportswear company launched a "feeling great,be great” campaign recently .The idea was simple but quite extraordinary, orchestrate a message along a tangent resonant with everyone across the world and consequently project appeal as a company that understands its clientele.It had an effect.-the box office didn't disappoint as usual. The essence of corporate storytelling is immersing your messaging into the behavioral complex of your most alignable customer AKA,the mass consumer and curating your actions based on that body of thought. Over time you may realize that “you get to lose one of your “best potential customers” because of the wrong tangent of approach in presenting yourself and your aesthetic in the proposal Stage.
